ISBM College of Engineering Pune 
    ISBM College of Engineering (also seen as "ISB&M College of Engineering") is a private engineering institute affiliated 
    with Savitribai Phule Pune University and accredited with a NAAC grade (B++). It offers undergraduate program 
    like B.E. in Computer Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Artifical Intelligence & Machine Learing with a curriculum 
    aligned with university regulations.
    

    

    Academic Environment
    
    Faculty & Teaching :
    Faculty members are reported as qualified and supportive by many students; they help clarify concepts and assist learners.
    Some reviews mention frequent faculty turnover and varied teaching quality across subjects.
    Curriculum and exams follow a strict academic pattern under Savitribai Phule Pune University, with emphasis on theory
    and practical application.

    Classroom & Learning Support:
    Regular lectures, industrial visits, and project-based learning help reinforce engineering fundamentals.
    Infrastructure like labs and libraries are generally functional, though some students feel they could be improved 
    further.Attendance requirements and exam rigor push students to stay consistent academically but can be challenging for some.

    Culture & Activities:
    The college conducts fests, extracurricular events and workshops which support overall student development.
